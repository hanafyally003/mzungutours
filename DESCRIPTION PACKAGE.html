Experience the best of Tanzania’s wildlife on this unforgettable safari covering Ngorongoro Crater and Serengeti National Park. Witness the Big Five, endless savannahs, and breathtaking landscapes with professional guides.

Day 2 – Serengeti National Park

Full-day safari exploring the endless plains and rich wildlife.

Day 3 – Ngorongoro Crater

Descend into the crater for an unforgettable Big Five experience.
