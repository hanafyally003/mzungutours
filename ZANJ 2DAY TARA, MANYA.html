2 Days 1 Night Safari From Zanzibar: Tarangire & Lake Manyara

Join us on a breathtaking journey through two of Tanzania’s most iconic wildlife destinations Tarangire National Park and Lake Manyara. This tour offers a perfect combination of immersive safari experiences, rich wildlife encounters, and serene landscapes. With our expertly guided game drives, you’ll have the chance to witness Tanzania’s diverse ecosystems and encounter majestic animals in their natural habitats. Animals like Elephants, Zebras, Giraffe, Flamingo birds and many more. Whether you’re a wildlife enthusiast or simply looking for a serene escape into nature, this two-day adventure promises unforgettable moments.

Tour Highlights

  • Day 1: explore wild life at Tarangire
  • Day 2: explore wild life at Lake mmanyara & Return
DURATION: HALF DAY PRICE: $1415/PERSON

Tour Overview

Day 1: explore wild life at Tarangire
Early morning pick-up from your hotel in Zanzibar. Transfer to Zanzibar Airport for a domestic flight to Arusha Airport. Upon arrival, transfer to Tarangire National Park for an exciting safari game drive. Tarangire is famous for its large elephant populations and towering baobab trees. A packed lunch will be provided to enjoy during the drive. In the evening, transfer to Africa Safari Lake Manyara for an overnight stay in a comfortable tent. Full board (FB) services included.

Day 2: explore wild life at Lake manyara & Return
After breakfast, enjoy a full-day safari game drive in Lake Manyara National Park, known for its tree-climbing lions, rich birdlife, and stunning scenery. Return in the evening to Africa Safari Lake Manyara for another night in your comfort tent with full board service.
Start your day with an early breakfast and check-out from your accommodation. Enjoy a morning safari game drive in Lake Manyara National Park, known for its tree-climbing lions, vibrant birdlife, and stunning lake views. A packed lunch will be provided. After the game drive, transfer to Arusha Airport for an afternoon flight back to Zanzibar. Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel.

About This tour

This 5 Days 4 Nights safari from Zanzibar covers Tarangire, Lake Manyara, offering rich wildlife experiences, Big Five sightings, scenic landscapes, and cultural encounters, all in one unforgettable Tanzania safari adventure.
