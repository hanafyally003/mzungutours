Mnemba Snorkeling Tour

Mnemba Island is a top snorkeling destination in Zanzibar, known for its vibrant coral reefs and abundant marine life. Protected from fishing, the waters are a safe haven for colorful fish, starfish, snappers, Moorish idols, green turtles, and more, offering an unforgettable underwater experience.

Tour Highlights

  • Swimming and Snorkeling around Mnemba corals
  • Seafoods BBQ Lunch; Octopus, Lobsters, Squids, Prawns, etc.Extra Costs
  • Tropical Fruits; Mangoes, Bananas, Watermelon etc.
  • Sailing with Traditional boat
  • Sometimes you might spot Dolphins, but not always
DURATION: FULL DAY PRICE: $55/PERSON

About mnemba island

Is a small private island off Matemwe Beach, northeast Zanzibar, surrounded by a vibrant reef. About 500 meters wide and 1.5 km in circumference, it’s ideal for snorkeling, scuba diving, and dolphin watching, offering privacy and a perfect getaway for visitors

Seafoods BBQ

During the Mnemba Island full-day tour, You will have Seafood BBQ Lunch features freshly grilled seafood like lobster, prawns, octopus, squid, and fish, served with tropical fruits offering a delicious and relaxing meal by the beach or on the boat.
